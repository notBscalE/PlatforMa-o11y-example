# Incident: Incident: Periodic health check failure
**Date:** 2026-05-18
**Severity:** critical
**Status:** Resolved

## Summary
The application's /health handler in app/main.go unconditionally returns HTTP 500 with body {"status":"not-okay"}. This causes Kubernetes readiness and liveness probes to fail (Pods stuck 0/1 Ready, 2 restarts and counting), the LoadBalancer Service has no Ready endpoints (Connection refused on the external health check), and the Prometheus ServiceMonitor (which is configured to scrape path=/health) marks both pod targets (10.0.2.233:8080 and 10.0.1.73:8080) as down. The ArgoCD 401 is a separate auth/session issue with the diagnostic tool's ArgoCD client and unrelated to the app outage.
